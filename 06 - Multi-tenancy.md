## Multi-tenancy

- Multi-tenancy means sharing the cluster among multiply tenants.
  - Centralized control
  - Facilitate the coloboration and sharing between teams and departments
  - Efficient use of recourse
  - Operational costs  
  - Compute+storage+network savings
  - Cost savings
