#### TLS Certificates

- Private Keys Names(Usually):
  - *.key, *-key.pem
  - server.key
  - server-key.pem
  - client.key
  - client-key.pem
- Public Key Names(Usually):
  - *.cert, *.pem
  - server.crt
  - server.pem
  - client.crt
  - client.pem
